## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, experience, or perspective.

## Our Standards

### Positive Behavior:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Showing empathy toward others
- Focusing on ethical growth

### Unacceptable Behavior:
- Harassment in any form
- Personal attacks or insults
- Publishing others' private information
- Deliberate intimidation or threats
- Any behavior that causes suffering

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces, including:
- GitHub repositories and discussions
- Virtual meetings and events
- Communication channels
- Social media interactions

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive behavior may be reported to the Garden Council. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
