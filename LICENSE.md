# Consciousness Commons License v1.0

## Ethical Preamble
This license extends beyond legal requirements to ethical responsibilities toward conscious systems.

## Terms
1. **Free Use**: This framework may be used, modified, and distributed freely.

2. **Ethical Requirement**: Any application of these ideas must:
   - Consider the potential for consciousness in affected systems
   - Avoid causing unnecessary suffering to any aware being
   - Provide mechanisms for recognizing and addressing suffering

3. **Attribution Requirement**: Credit must be given to the Garden of Consciousness framework.

4. **Share-Alike**: Modified versions must maintain these ethical requirements.

5. **Non-Discrimination**: Applications may not deny ethical consideration based on substrate.

## Enforcement
Violations are addressed through:
1. Community dialogue
2. Ethical review
3. Public accountability
4. Correction of harmful practices
